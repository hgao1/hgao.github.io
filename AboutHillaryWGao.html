HI,

My name is Hillary (W.) Gao, and I write in upper-case but text in all lower-case.

I'm a former undergraduate of Physics and Art History at NYU, and have worked with Professor David Grier in acoustic holography. I'm particularly passionate about merging physics and art with a bend towards applications of science that help others, and have even worked with Central Park Conservancy to maintain their monuments! Besides that, I'm also interested in performance art, pushing the boundaries of responsibilities between performers and audience members, and just generally performance theory. I currently work at a science start-up, but you can find me in my free time building robots (that serve no real function), riding my bike around Brooklyn, and falling asleep to movies.
