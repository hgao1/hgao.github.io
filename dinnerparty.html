WOULD YOU SET THE TABLE IF I ASKED YOU TO?

"would you set the table if I asked you to?" came from a reaction towards the surge in hate crimes towards Asian Americans during the COVID-19 Pandemic. The piece serves to act as a mirror to the audience, asking them to re-examine their participation, perception, and place at an otherwise civilized dinner party.
